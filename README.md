 NHS England A&E Performance Analysis — March 2025

Tools:SQL (SQLite) · Tableau · Excel  
Data: NHS England public dataset — 187 trusts, March 2025  

 Project Summary
Independent end-to-end analysis of A&E performance across every 
NHS trust in England, built on publicly available NHS England data. 
Covers national performance, trust-level RAG classification, 
regional comparison, and 12-hour wait risk.

 Key Findings
- National 4-hour performance: 73.8% against a 95% target
- 91 trusts (49%) critically failing — classified RED
- North West worst-performing region at 76.1% average
- 116 out of 187 trusts recorded patients waiting over 12 hours
- Shrewsbury & Telford highest risk at 9.94% 12-hour wait rate
